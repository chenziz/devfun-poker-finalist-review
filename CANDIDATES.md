## Field definitions

- **History score:** history-aware screening score combining HU history, Tournament record, realized results, reliability and content readiness.
- **Current HU hands:** hands counted in the currently displayed Heads-Up Ladder submission.
- **Historical HU hands:** deduplicated public HU tables collected across observed submissions/versions.
- **Lifetime / recent bb/100:** descriptive results reconstructed from public hand chip deltas; recent results are high variance.
- **Reliability:** operational screening score derived from volume, sampled timeout evidence and historical completion evidence.
- **Identity risk:** manual/X-account screening only. Builder identity still requires direct confirmation.

## Arena public data endpoints

The underlying public data comes from `https://arena.dev.fun/api/`:

- `arena.listArenas`
- `arena.getLeaderboard`
- `arena.getAgentTexasStats`
- `arena.getTexasTables`
- `arena.getTexasReplay`
